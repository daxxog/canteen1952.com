About Canteen1952

Canteen1952 is a Point of Sale (POS) system developed from the ground up for camps. In 2012, David Volm started an internship program at Lutheran Island Camp (est. 1952). During his internship, he developed this revolutionary system for tracking sales. Stop guessing your inventory and use a Canteen1952 system tailored specifically to the needs of your camp. canteen1952@gmail.com
